The application is named `IntelGram`. Its distinct bundle and application IDs let it coexist with a normal AyuGram installation. The macOS package is ad-hoc signed, so macOS may require Control-click, then **Open**, on first launch.

After signing in, open **IntelGram Settings -> Other -> Local profile**. The local display name, UID, username list, anonymous number, photo, clone, and collectible settings only affect how your own account is rendered in IntelGram. The main search field can also find already-loaded peers by UID or visible phone number. None of these additions mutate Telegram profile data, import contacts, or change collectible ownership.
